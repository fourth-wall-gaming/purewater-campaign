# Purewater Adventure: Key Character Summaries

## Primary Protagonists

### Gardwen
- **Role**: Druid seeking her long-lost brother
- **Background**: Survivor of a brutal Dragon Knight raid that killed her family 10 years ago
- **Abilities**: Nature/water magic, healing powers, animal companion (wolf named Ruhi)
- **Motivation**: To find her brother who was taken during the raid
- **Connection to Plot**: Her brother is the possessed warrior serving Baron Hanzo

### Magda
- **Role**: Elderly warrior woman serving the death goddess Hel
- **Background**: Former farmer called to action by prophetic dreams/visions
- **Abilities**: Combat prowess belying her age, limited prophetic abilities
- **Motivation**: Following Hel's directive to protect Gardwen and the twins
- **Quote**: "I swear by Hel herself, little one, we'll find him or find out what happened to him."

### Randall
- **Role**: Street thief from Purewater
- **Background**: Raised in a brothel on the Pearl Quarter, never knew his parents
- **Abilities**: Stealth, thievery, local connections
- **Secret**: Twin brother of Conall, biological son of Baron Hanzo
- **Connection to Plot**: His resemblance to Santo creates opportunities and dangers

### Conall
- **Role**: Magical investigator
- **Background**: Adopted by a family in Anminster, trained by the Order of the Blue Star
- **Abilities**: Arcane magic, investigation skills
- **Secret**: Twin brother of Randall, biological son of Baron Hanzo
- **Connection to Plot**: Working with Temerach Nebulo to expose the Baron

### Temerach Nebulo
- **Role**: "The Lakelady's Javelin," elven knight
- **Background**: Member of the Swords of the Lake, an elite knightly order
- **Abilities**: Exceptional skill with bow and spear
- **Motivation**: To expose and stop Baron Hanzo's threat to Mystamyr
- **Connection to Plot**: Has been tracking the Baron and has knowledge of his past crimes

## Primary Antagonists

### Baron Hanzo di Teufel
- **Role**: Main villain, practitioner of fire-based demonology
- **Background**: Rising political power in the borderlands, secretly pursuing dragon transformation
- **Abilities**: Fire magic, demonology, possession rituals
- **Motivation**: To gain political influence through tournament victory, ultimately seeking to become a Dragon King
- **Secret**: Father of the twins (Randall/Conall) through rape of an elven mage

### Santo di Teufel
- **Role**: Baron's cruel son
- **Background**: Trained in his father's dark arts
- **Abilities**: Limited fire magic, ritual knife skills
- **Motivation**: To prove himself to his father, personal sadistic pleasure
- **Connection to Plot**: Attempted ritual on Emmeralda mirrors father's larger plan

### Tat Atarer (Leader of the Dragon Knights)
- **Role**: Military commander of the Baron's forces
- **Background**: Former mercenary captain
- **Personality**: Professional, pragmatic, values discipline
- **Motivation**: Money and security, not ideological commitment
- **Quote**: "The Baron pays for our swords, not our opinions."

### Blau (Lieutenant of the Dragon Knights)
- **Role**: The Baron's enforcer for "dirty work"
- **Appearance**: Albino with red eyes, thin build but unnaturally strong
- **Abilities**: Developing fire magic abilities
- **Personality**: Cold, calculating, cruel
- **Secret**: Studying the Baron's techniques, possibly planning to break away

## Supporting Characters

### Thuban Eta
- **Role**: Ancient dragon knight spirit possessing Gardwen's brother
- **Background**: Skilled military leader betrayed by comrades in the Dragon army
- **Current State**: Reluctant participant in possession, held in thrall by Hanzo
- **Connection to Plot**: Key to the Baron's tournament plan
- **Weakness**: Control maintained through his earthly remains and a special locket

### Cailan (Gardwen's Brother)
- **Role**: The possessed warrior, Baron's tournament champion
- **Background**: Kidnapped during Dragon Knight raid as a child
- **Current State**: Experiences moments of clarity between periods of possession
- **Appearance**: Young man in ancient dragon-scale armor that partially fuses with his skin
- **Abilities**: Unnatural combat precision, minor fire abilities, ancient fighting techniques

### Emmeralda
- **Role**: Elven courtesan who survived Santo's ritual
- **Current State**: Partially possessed by a lesser pleasure demon
- **Significance**: Through her, a counter-ritual might be developed
- **Development**: Potential ally if her possession is addressed
- **Connection to Plot**: Living evidence of the ritual Santo attempted

### Ravella
- **Role**: Information broker who runs The Siren's Call
- **Background**: Half-merfolk heritage kept mostly secret
- **Resources**: Network of informants throughout Purewater
- **Motivation**: Maintaining balance between pure and corrupt water magic
- **Abilities**: Access to water-based information networks, possible water magic

### Constantine
- **Role**: Runs The Sylph's Embrace brothel
- **Personality**: Protective of her workers, fiercely independent
- **Connection to Plot**: Her establishment was the site of Santo's attack
- **Relationship**: Works under Marisette's overall authority

### Thorne
- **Role**: Storyteller at The Moist Oyster tavern
- **Secret Identity**: Former member of the Order of the Lake
- **Knowledge**: Historical information about Dragon Kings era
- **Resources**: Extensive network of informants
- **Value**: Can provide context about water/fire magic conflict

### Crowbill
- **Role**: Criminal underground leader
- **Operations**: Canal smuggling, gambling, protection rackets
- **Code**: Against murder of locals, harm to children, disruption of sacred sites
- **Resources**: Network of lookouts, hidden canal routes, safe houses
- **Quote**: "We may be thieves, but we're Purewater thieves."

### High Priestess Nerissa
- **Role**: Leader at the Temple of the Lake Lady
- **Abilities**: Sense water corruption, perform water-based rituals
- **Concern**: Tracking increasing corruption in sacred waters
- **Restriction**: Cannot directly intervene unless sacred sites are threatened
- **Value**: Provides spiritual support and ritual knowledge

## Important Items

### The Ritual Knife
- **Description**: Demonologist's soul knife with symbol of Asmodeus
- **Purpose**: Used to bind spirits to victims' flesh
- **Current Location**: Found at crime scene, potentially in PCs' possession
- **Significance**: Evidence connecting Santo to the crime, tool for ritual

### The Binding Locket
- **Description**: Contains part of the binding connection to Thuban Eta
- **Significance**: Critical component of the possession ritual
- **Plot Element**: Will be stolen before the tournament
- **Importance**: Baron would "turn the entire city inside out to retrieve it"

### Thuban's Remains
- **Description**: Bones of the ancient dragon knight
- **Location**: In Baron Hanzo's tower in the borderlands
- **Significance**: Primary anchor for the possession ritual
- **Plot Potential**: Destroying these might free Cailan

## Locations

### The Sylph's Embrace
- **Description**: High-end pleasure house run by Constantine
- **Significance**: Site of Santo's ritual attack on Emmeralda
- **Location**: Pearl Quarter

### The Siren's Call
- **Description**: Ravella's establishment, part brothel, part information market
- **Location**: Where High Isle's canals meet poorer districts
- **Significance**: Nexus of secrets and whispers

### The Moist Oyster
- **Description**: Rough tavern in the Maze district
- **Significance**: Gathering place for information, Thorne's usual location
- **Atmosphere**: Cheap drinks, loose tongues, occasional knife fights

### The DragonBarge
- **Description**: Baron's massive vessel that steams where it touches water
- **Location**: Docked at private pier on eastern side of High Isle
- **Contents**: Baron's quarters, magical apparatus, training area for possessed warrior

### The Tournament Grounds
- **Description**: Located on High Isle
- **Features**: Archery ranges, combat rings, Harbor Run course
- **Significance**: Central location for the adventure's climax
