The eastern point of [[High Isle]] -- the city's industrial belt. Forges, foundries,
boatyards, tanneries and metalworks crowd the seaward channel here, many of them
**Dwarven-run**, their chimneys trailing smoke over the water. The quarter grinds
on day and night and spills naturally eastward into the docks of [[The Shoals]]. It
is also where the Baron's **[[The DragonBarge]]** lies at its private pier, steam
boiling off the channel where fire-magic meets the Lake's water -- the corruption
worst, and the heat most felt, in these eastern yards. (Formerly described as a
separate "Forge Isle"; it is in truth a quarter of High Isle.)
