A tangled warren of narrow canals and crooked streets, the beating heart of Purewater's street life and its thieves. Easy to get lost in -- and dangerous to *look* lost in, as [[Conall]] learns when he is cornered in a cul-de-sac by [[Sinnit]] and four blades within an hour of exploring. Some hold that the Maze's canal-patterns form natural magical sigils, and that folk magic worked at certain "power corners" runs stronger; local practitioners guard the knowledge. The [[The Moist Oyster]] sits along a street called the Serpentine.
