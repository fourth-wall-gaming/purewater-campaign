One of [[The Northern Isles]]: a half-sunken district of drowned, abandoned buildings
and ancient ruins where the Lake has flowed undisturbed for generations, weed-choked
and silent. Animists hold that powerful spirits have taken up residence here, which
makes it both perilous and prized by spirit-binders -- and somewhere beneath these
waters lie the hidden chambers of [[The Mermaid's Court]]. Mysterious, dangerous,
and largely unexplored; the boatmen give it a wide berth.
