A partially sunken, abandoned district of drowned buildings and ancient ruins where water has flowed undisturbed for decades. Animists hold that powerful spirits have taken up residence here, making it both perilous and prized for spirit-binders -- and beneath it lie the hidden chambers of [[The Mermaid's Court]]. Mysterious, dangerous, and largely unexplored.
