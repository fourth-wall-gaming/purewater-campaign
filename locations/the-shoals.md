The **seaward jaw of the Mouth**: a peninsula reaching in from the north-east to
form the eastern side of the strait, where the Lake's outflow finally meets the
**open sea**. This is Purewater's working waterfront -- fish-markets and wharves,
ship-chandlers and net-lofts, the **Harbor Masters' tower** that governs the lawful
traffic, and the half-hidden **smugglers' jetties** that govern the rest.

The Shoals face the sea and take its weather; ancestor-spirits of drowned sailors
are honoured here. The quarter draws much of its life from the neighbouring
[[The Lullwater]] just inland and west, and the two are bound by trade both honest
and not. The [[The Dredgers]] are strong on these docks; the [[The Harbor Masters]]
officially rule them while quietly tolerating the contraband that keeps the whole
waterfront fed.
