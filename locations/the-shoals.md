The dockside fringe of Purewater: fishermen, dock workers, and the less-regulated maritime trade, which is to say smuggling. Ancestor-spirits of drowned sailors are honored here, and the [[The Dredgers]] draw much of their strength from these wharves and the neighboring [[The Warren]]. The [[The Harbor Masters]] officially rule the docks; the underworld quietly runs the contraband.
