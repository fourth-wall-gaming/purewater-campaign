The heart of Purewater: a large, irregular, roughly **triangular** island wedged in
the Mouth, splitting the sacred Lake's outflow around it. Its three points anchor
the city -- the **south-west** point reaching toward [[Caravan Square]] across the
landward channel, the **east** point toward [[The Shoals]] across the seaward
channel, and the **north-west** point facing the open Lake.

The **Grand Canal** does not ring the isle so much as **bisect and branch across
it**, a web of broad cut channels that carve the island into natural quarters and
carry barge-traffic to every door:

- **The [[The Merchant's Quarter]]** -- the **south-west** corner, by the landward
  channel and the caravan trade: counting-houses, guild halls, the Watercrafters,
  the great market plaza, and the palazzi of the merchant princes who sit on the
  city council.
- **The official & noble quarter** -- the **north-east**, the prestigious frontage
  facing the Lake and [[Temple Isle]]: the **Governor's Palazzo** (seat of
  [[Prince Emeric]]), the **Hall of Justice**, the **Barracks**, and **Palace
  Square**, ringed by the houses of the nobility and the city's wealthiest (though,
  in truth, few of the realm's *truly* powerful keep more than a token residence
  here).
- **The [[The Forge Quarter]] & the industrial yards** -- the **east** point:
  forges, foundries, tanneries and boatyards trailing smoke, the gritty working
  belt that spills naturally eastward into [[The Shoals]].
- **The residential northern edge** -- broad avenues run **east-west** across the
  isle, from the Merchant's Quarter to the forges. Along the **northern edge**,
  moving south-east, the houses slope steadily from **rich to middling** until they
  reach a canal-mouth opening to the Lake -- and **across that canal lies [[The
  Lullwater]]**, the crowded poor-and-criminal quarter.

The Baron's **[[The DragonBarge]]** lies at a private pier on the isle's **eastern,
seaward side**, steam rising where its hull meets the water. The **Tournament
Grounds** -- archery ranges firing across open water, the combat lists, and the
start of the Harbor Run -- occupy the open ground between Palace Square and the
quays, the stage for the campaign's climax.
