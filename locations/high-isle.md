The central and grandest island, separated from its neighbors by the Grand Canal. Here sit the Governor's seat and government buildings, the major temples to the Lake Lady, the residences of the wealthy and noble -- and the grounds of the **Lake Lady's Tourney**.

The tournament grounds hold archery ranges that fire across open water at targets on boats and the far canal-banks, combat rings for the dueling lists (where the Baron means to enter his champion), and the start of the **Harbor Run** obstacle course that winds through the channels and over the rooftops. It is the stage for the campaign's climax: the first public display of the possessed champion [[Cailan]], and the moment [[Gardwen]] recognizes her brother across the tournament ground. The Baron's [[The DragonBarge]] lies at a private pier on the island's eastern side.
