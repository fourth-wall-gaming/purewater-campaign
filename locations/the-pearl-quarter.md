The entertainment district of the Pleasure Islands, named for the lamps its houses string like pearls along the black canal water. Its central waterfront street, the **Pearl Quay**, is lined with houses high and low -- the Lily Garden, the Promise of Heaven, the Aphrodisia House, [[The Sylph's Embrace]], and the grander [[The Siren's Call]]. Legitimate entertainment and criminal trade braid together here, all under the near-absolute authority of the madame [[Marisette]].

[[Randall]] was raised here, in the Promise of Heaven, looked after by the working women who taught him to be useful and funny enough to survive. It is also the scene of the crime that opens the campaign: [[Santo di Teufel]]'s botched binding ritual on the courtesan [[Emmeralda]] at the Sylph's Embrace, three nights before the tourney.

*(Setting note: the Pearl Quarter replaces the Zorastor draft's "Street of Red Lanterns," a Thieves' World / Sanctuary throwback.)*
