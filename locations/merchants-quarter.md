The south-west quarter of [[High Isle]], where the landward channel and the road
from [[Caravan Square]] bring the city's trade ashore. Here stand the guild halls
and counting-houses, the **Watercrafters' Guild**, the great market plaza, and the
palazzi of the **merchant princes** who handle Purewater's day-to-day governance
from the city council. Wealthy, busy, and politically slippery -- this is where the
city's money is made, weighed, and quietly steered. (Formerly described as a
separate "Merchant's Isle"; in the city's true layout it is a quarter of High Isle,
divided from the rest by a branch of the Grand Canal.)
