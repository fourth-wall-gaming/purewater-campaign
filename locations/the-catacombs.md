A network of tunnels, caverns, and centuries-old water-pools beneath Purewater, older than the city above. Smugglers use them as hidden routes and the underworld uses them for meetings -- and for darker work; to be "taken to the catacombs" is to be in mortal trouble. It was here that [[Marisette]]'s people dragged [[Randall]] and [[Conall]], bound to chairs and a hairsbreadth from having their throats cut, before the truth of Santo's crime came out and the four strangers' alliance was forged.

The untouched pools have developed strange magical properties, and some believe they connect to primordial water sources predating Purewater itself -- making the catacombs of interest to animists, the [[The Mermaid's Court]], and anyone seeking the city's oldest secrets.
