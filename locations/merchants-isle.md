The commercial heart of Purewater: guild halls, trade organizations, and the grand residences of the merchant princes who sit on the city council and handle its day-to-day governance. The [[The Watercrafters Guild]] keeps its principal workshops here. Wealth and trade-politics concentrate on this island as power and faith do on [[High Isle]] and [[Temple Isle]].
