Baron Hanzo's personal vessel: a massive ship adorned with dragon motifs that **steams continuously where its hull meets the water**, fire magic warring with the sacred canals and slowly corrupting the channels around its private pier on the eastern side of [[High Isle]]. It is a mobile focus for his fire demonology and the environmental sign of his presence -- the corruption spreading from it is what alarms the water-faiths.

Within: the Baron's quarters and magical apparatus, fire-demonology workspaces, a training area for the possessed champion [[Cailan]], and documents revealing his political aspirations and the [[The Awake the Dragon Movement]]. Guarded by Dragon Knights and well-defended, but not impregnable -- a key infiltration target. (Note: the Baron also keeps to a fortified estate east of town in some tellings; the DragonBarge is his floating seat of power in Purewater.)
