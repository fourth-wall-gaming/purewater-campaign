An elegant, high-end pleasure house run by [[Constantine]], its walls thick enough to muffle the night's business. [[Magda]] took work here protecting the girls, and brought [[Gardwen]] in to stay.

Two nights after their arrival it became a crime scene: a patron resembling [[Randall]] carved a long, curling ritual wound the length of the courtesan [[Emmeralda]]'s body before fleeing, dropping his soul-knife behind an armoire. Gardwen's druidic healing saved the girl from death -- and from complete possession by the pleasure demon Santo was trying to bind. The house now keeps extra security and a wary eye on the street; Constantine wants justice, and the demon still stirs in Emmeralda's flesh.
