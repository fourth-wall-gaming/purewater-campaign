A small island set apart off the **north-east** edge of the city, out in the Lake's
clean waters -- **deliberately unbridged** to [[High Isle]] and reached only by the
temple's own ferries, so that the profane city cannot simply walk to the holy
ground. It is given over almost entirely to the **temple of the Lake Lady** and the
**Sacred Confluence**: a great round sacred pool, fed where the purest lake-water
wells up, where divine magic runs strongest and the goddess is said to manifest at
high ceremony.

Here [[High Priestess Nerissa]] tends the waters and tracks the corruption creeping
north from the DragonBarge; here the [[The Order of the Lake Lady]] keeps its rites.

> **For the mapmaker:** unlike the dense house-by-house districts, Temple Isle should
> be rendered as **detailed, deliberate sacred architecture** -- a planned
> ground-plan of the temple precinct: the colonnaded approach, the temple itself, the
> ring of lesser shrines, and the geometry of the Confluence pool and its channels
> and basins. A formal architectural layout, not a tangle of rooftops.
