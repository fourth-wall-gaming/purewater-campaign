The oldest sacred ground in Purewater, crowded with shrines and the chief temples of the [[The Order of the Lake Lady]]. Its holiest site is the **Sacred Confluence** -- a carefully tended pool where the purest lake water meets the sea, where divine magic runs strongest and the Lake Lady is said to manifest during high ceremonies.

It is here that the city's water corruption is most keenly felt and fought: [[High Priestess Nerissa]] tracks the rising pollution from the Confluence, and the Order's rituals of purification are centered here. As the Baron's fire magic spreads, the water spirits withdraw and the Confluence's power flickers -- a slow desecration that temple law leaves Nerissa unable to answer directly.
