The island of Purewater's Dwarven craftsmen and artisans: forges, metalworking, and the manufacture of quality goods. Their smiths are rivals to the [[The Watercrafters Guild]] -- competing over the city's craft trade -- though collaborative projects sometimes bridge the two.
