North of [[High Isle]], where the Lake widens into its calmer, cleaner reaches, lies
a **scatter of small irregular islands** -- the gentler, stranger half of Purewater,
away from the crush of the Mouth.

- **[[The Pearl]]** -- the bright one: pleasure-houses and high nightlife.
- **[[Lost Isle]]** -- ruins, some of them half-drowned, weed-choked and deserted;
  the hidden chambers of [[The Mermaid's Court]] lie somewhere beneath the Northern
  Isles.
- **Court retreats** -- several islets are owned by court families as private
  island-villas and garden-retreats. (Worth remembering: comparatively **few of the
  realm's truly powerful** actually live in Purewater; many of these "noble"
  retreats are minor houses, faded names, or shuttered most of the year.)
- **The shunned isles** -- and some are simply avoided. One is said to be **haunted**;
  another was long ago **claimed by some water-power or magic** and is no longer
  quite of the mortal city; a third sank within living memory and now shows only
  rooftops and a drowned bell at low water. Boatmen know which channels not to take
  after dark.

The Northern Isles reward exploration: a place for retreats and trysts and quiet
deals, for ruins and water-spirits, and for mysteries the bustling city below the
Mouth has half-forgotten.
