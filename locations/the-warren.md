Purewater's poor district: crowded working-class tenements and a street-level survival economy. It is the heartland of the [[The Dredgers]], whose guildhall and safe houses shelter the canal-laborers, fishers, and working poor, and where [[Marda Blackwater]]'s movement for worker solidarity burns hottest against the merchant princes and the Baron alike.
