The brightest of [[The Northern Isles]]: a small island north of [[High Isle]],
given over to pleasure and the high end of Purewater's nightlife. Its houses string
their lamps like pearls along the waterfront **Pearl Quay** -- the [[The Sylph's
Embrace]], the grander [[The Siren's Call]], the Aphrodisia House, the Lily Garden,
and the Promise of Heaven (where [[Randall]] was raised among its women). Legitimate
entertainment and quiet crime braid together here, all under the near-absolute
authority of the madame [[Marisette]].

Set apart on its own island, lamplit and lovely over dark water, the Pearl is where
the city's powerful come to be indiscreet -- which makes it, after the [[The
Currents]], the richest seam of secrets in Purewater. It is also the scene of the
crime that opens the campaign: [[Santo di Teufel]]'s botched binding ritual on the
courtesan [[Emmeralda]] at the Sylph's Embrace, three nights before the tourney.

*(Formerly "the Street of Red Lanterns" / "the Pearl Quarter"; now its own island.)*
