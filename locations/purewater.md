Purewater is built across **the Mouth**: the narrow choke-point where the sacred
**Lake** to the north pours its holy waters south into the **open Sea**. It is the
gateway between inland lake-trade and the ocean routes -- rich, strategic, and
rotten. Locals call it **"Pisswater"** for the pollution and the corruption alike.

**The shape of the place** (north = the Lake; south = the Sea; west = the mainland):

- From the **western** mainland, a thin **promontory** reaches eastward and ends in
  a larger outcrop -- **[[Caravan Square]]** and the **Gate of Triumph**, the city's
  only land approach. This is the southern jaw of the Mouth.
- A second **peninsula**, set a little to the north and east, reaches in to form the
  seaward jaw. On it sit **[[The Shoals]]** -- the working docks, fish-markets and
  smugglers' jetties facing the open sea.
- Between the two jaws sits **[[High Isle]]**, a great irregular triangular island
  that splits the Lake's outflow. Its south-west point reaches toward Caravan Square
  across a calmer **landward channel**; its east point reaches toward the Shoals
  across the **seaward channel** (the true Mouth); its north-west point faces the
  open Lake. The waters part around the isle and rejoin to the south.
- North of High Isle, scattered across the Lake's calmer waters, lie **[[The Northern
  Isles]]** -- among them the bright pleasure-island of **[[The Pearl]]**, the
  half-drowned ruins of **[[Lost Isle]]**, quiet court-family retreats, and a few
  shunned, haunted, or magically claimed islets. Off the north-east edge, alone and
  unbridged, sits little **[[Temple Isle]]** with its temple and Sacred Confluence.

Gondolas and barges are the streets; bridges stitch the islands; mist and steam,
salt and canal-stink fill the air. Under it all runs the campaign's elemental
tension -- the Lake Lady's pure waters against the fire-corruption bleeding from the
Baron's **[[The DragonBarge]]**, moored on High Isle's seaward side. Beneath
everything run **[[The Catacombs]]**.
