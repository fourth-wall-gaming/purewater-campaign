[[Ravella]]'s establishment, sited where High Isle's canals meet the poorer districts: part high-end pleasure house, part information market, and one of the few patches of neutral ground in Purewater. Here the half-merfolk broker holds court and runs her network of gondoliers, washerwomen, and dockhands -- everyone who touches water daily -- trading in secrets as much as pleasure. Whispered deals between rival factions happen under its roof; it is the city's great clearinghouse of information, and a likely doorway to the merfolk paths beneath Purewater.
