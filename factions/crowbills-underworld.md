Purewater's criminal underground: canal smuggling, gambling dens, and protection rackets, run by [[Crowbill]] -- a wiry, forgettable man whose only distinctive mark is a small crow tattoo on the back of his right hand. His people signal with crow symbols, and he keeps hidden boat-paths through the canals and safe houses across the city.

What sets the underworld apart is its **code**: no murder of locals, no harm to children, no disruption of the Lake Lady's sacred sites. (In the older Zorastor telling this was Jubal's Talons and the thief Randall's home turf; in Purewater the role is Crowbill's, given an explicit ethic.) Crowbill was content to profit from the Baron's arrival at first, but the Dragon Knights' brutality is wrecking the equilibrium his business depends on -- which is steadily turning him into a possible ally. [[Randall]], [[Nus]], [[Asphodel]] and [[Mariarta]] all move within this world.
