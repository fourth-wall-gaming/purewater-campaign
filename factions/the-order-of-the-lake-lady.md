Both a religious cult and a civic power, the Order maintains the temples and sacred pools of Purewater (chiefly on [[Temple Isle]]) and performs the rituals that keep the increasingly polluted waters pure. Members wear blue-green robes worked with silver waves.

**Ranks:** Water Initiates, Reflectionists, Tide Wardens, Wave Speakers (those with proven water-affinity), Deep Visionaries (who commune with the Lake Lady), and The High Tide -- the ruling council of five Deep Visionaries.

**Leadership.** [[Lilura Deepcurrent]] has led the Order for three decades: elderly, piercing-eyed, rumored to carry merfolk ancestry. Beneath her, [[High Priestess Nerissa]] runs the Temple of the Lake Lady day to day -- she can sense water corruption, bless sacred waters, and speak with water spirits, but temple law forbids her from intervening directly unless the sacred sites are threatened. She therefore needs agents.

**Stated aim:** preserve the sacred waters and the Lake Lady's blessings. **Real aim:** hold their political power as the waters fail; some higher clergy fear the goddess's power is waning and quietly study the corruption to understand it. Allied with the [[The Watercrafters Guild]] and on arranged terms with [[Ravella]]; their chief enemy is the Baron's fire demonology. Since his arrival the corruption -- and their alarm -- has steadily risen.
