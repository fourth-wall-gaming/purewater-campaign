The body that controls maritime traffic in and out of Purewater: docks, berths, tariffs, and the enforcement of maritime law. They work from a harbor tower and dockside offices, in dark blue uniforms badged by rank.

**Ranks:** Dockhands, Pilots, Harbormen, Tide Lords, Port Captains, and the seven-master Anchor Council.

**Leadership.** [[Harbormaster Velen Deepkeel]] -- stern, weathered, forty years risen through the ranks; meticulous and traditional, publicly incorruptible.

**Stated aim:** safe, efficient harbors and the city's standing as a trade hub. **Real aim:** to keep their near-monopoly on maritime commerce and its wealth; the upper ranks are increasingly corrupt, taking bribes to wave through smuggling while parading strict regulation. Strong ties to the Governor, council, and shipping merchants; an uneasy secret arrangement with [[Crowbill]]; open contempt for the [[The Dredgers]]; and a wary, opportunistic eye on the Baron.
