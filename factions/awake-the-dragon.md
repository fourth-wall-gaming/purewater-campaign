Beyond Baron Hanzo himself lies the cause he serves: a movement to revive the magic of the ancient Dragon Kings and -- ultimately -- to transform chosen men into dragons. The Baron's possession ritual, binding the ancient dragon-knight spirit [[Thuban Eta]] into a living vessel, is an early proof of this art: an attempt to recreate the draconic transformations of an age the realm thought destroyed in the old uprising.

How wide the movement runs is unknown -- it may have members across the realm, in other cities suffering their own corruptions. To the water-faiths and the [[The Mermaid's Court]] it is an existential threat: the return of an ancient order of fire and dominion against the older, pre-human powers of water. The Baron's victory at the Lake Lady's Tourney is meant to be the movement's first foothold in Purewater. **(GM:** see the GM-secrets lore for how the movement connects to the Baron's tower, Thuban's bones, and the wider campaign.)
