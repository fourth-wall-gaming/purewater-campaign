Ostensibly a noble martial order, the Dragon Knights are in truth Baron Hanzo di Teufel's instrument of conquest: hardened raiders and murderers who have terrorized the borderlands for thirty years. They wear armor emblazoned with dragons and many carry fire-touched weapons. Roughly twenty to thirty strong in Purewater, well-disciplined and thoroughly violent.

**Command.** The mercenary captain [[Tat Atarer]] is the leader of record -- a professional disciplinarian who fights for pay, not ideology ("the Baron pays for our swords, not our opinions"). Day-to-day operations and the Baron's dirty work fall to his albino lieutenant [[Blau]], known to the ranks as Commander Vask: burn-scarred, raspy-voiced, utterly loyal in public while secretly studying the Baron's techniques to one day break away.

**Ranks** (per the order's own conceit): Ember Recruits, Flame Soldiers, Smoke Lieutenants, Charred Captains, Ash Lords (elites who have undergone partial fire-binding), and the Dragon Circle (the Baron's inner guard).

**Stated aim:** keep order in the Baron's territories and honor the order through tournament display. **Real aim:** use the Lake Lady's Tourney as cover to plant a foothold in Purewater, disrupt its water magic, and extend Baron Hanzo's reach. Their worst enemies are those who survived their past raids -- [[Gardwen]] foremost among them. They are barracked in a fortified estate east of the city; the Baron himself keeps to the [[The DragonBarge]].
