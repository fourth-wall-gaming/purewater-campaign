An intelligence network and loose guild of independent operators who trade in secrets and favors rather than goods. They keep no headquarters, meeting at sites marked by chalk drawings of flowing water and identifying one another by coded talk of tides and currents.

**Ranks:** Droplets, Streams, Rivers, Confluences, Deltas, and the inner Watershed. There is no single head; the nearest thing is **"The Source,"** a hidden consortium of three to five unknown individuals.

**Stated aim:** keep information flowing and prevent any one faction dominating. **Real aim:** to be the true power behind Purewater, playing factions against each other to a balance that serves their unknown masters -- whom some believe sit in the capital or abroad. They deal with everyone -- [[Ravella]], [[Crowbill]], temple, and government alike -- and ally closely with no one. Their rivals are other spy networks, including Ravella's personal agents and the Baron's.
