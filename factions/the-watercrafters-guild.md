The guild of boat-builders, canal engineers, water-diviners, and makers of water-enhanced goods -- the artisans whose enchantments keep the canal-city functioning. They wear indigo sashes, rank marked in silver, blue, or mother-of-pearl.

**Ranks:** Apprentices, Journeymen, Master Crafters, Tide Masters, and a governing council of seven.

**Leadership.** [[Guildmaster Torval Bluehand]] -- middle-aged, practical, forward-thinking, his hands stained permanently blue by decades of watercraft enchantment. He prizes innovation over tradition.

**Stated aim:** advance the craft and protect guild members. **Real aim:** monopolize water technology and magic in Purewater; they secretly research how to harness the power released where lake and sea waters mix -- and study the Baron's [[The DragonBarge]] as both a threat and a fascinating innovation. Allied with merchants and the [[The Order of the Lake Lady]]; rivals of the Dwarven crafters of [[Forge Isle]].
