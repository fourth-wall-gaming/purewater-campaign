A hidden society of water-magic practitioners -- humans, half-merfolk, and the water-touched -- preserving the oldest forms of pure water magic, from chambers beneath [[Lost Isle]] and the canals. They leave little trace, known only by mother-of-pearl tokens and faint blue-green markings.

**Ranks:** Surface Swimmers, Tide Dancers, Depth Callers, Wave Shapers, Abyss Touched (transformed elites who can breathe water for hours), and a seven-member Deep Council. Their leader is known only as **"The Pearl"** -- merfolk princess, ancient elemental in human shape, or something else; few have met them.

**Stated aim:** protect the purest water magic and the bond with merfolk. **Real aim:** to reclaim Purewater, over generations if need be, and cleanse what they see as a spiritual as well as physical corruption. They are fundamentally opposed to the Baron's fire magic but favor patient sabotage over open war. [[Ravella]], the half-merfolk broker, may have ties to them.
