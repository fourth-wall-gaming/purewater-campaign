A magical order of arcane practitioners -- a true institution, with a hall, ranks, and a doctrine -- recognizable by the blue star that blazes at a master's forehead. They represent institutional magical authority and take a long, careful interest in dangerous power falling into the wrong hands.

It was the Blue Star wizard **Old Johz** who recognized [[Conall]] as the Baron's son when he was a boy in Anminster, healed him years later in Sharna, and trained him in arcane magic -- "so that power didn't fall into the wrong hands" -- before delivering him to [[Temerach Nebulo]] and abruptly departing. The order may intervene directly against the Baron, and is a likely source of counsel on the possession ritual and the demon afflicting [[Emmeralda]].

**A note on Hesper.** Because the Blue Star is the most visible mage-order in these parts, the sorcerer [[Hesper]] -- Marisette's enforcer in the Pearl Quarter -- is sometimes loosely assumed to be one of its wizards. Hesper is **not** of the Blue Star, and the assumption suits Hesper fine. *(GM: Hesper's true allegiance lies elsewhere -- see [[The Quiet Hand]].)*
