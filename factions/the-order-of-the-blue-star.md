A magical order of arcane practitioners, recognizable by the blue star that blazes at a master's forehead. They represent institutional magical authority and take a long, careful interest in dangerous power falling into the wrong hands.

It was the Blue Star wizard **Old Johz** who recognized [[Conall]] as the Baron's son when he was a boy in Anminster, healed him years later in Sharna, and trained him in arcane magic -- "so that power didn't fall into the wrong hands" -- before delivering him to [[Temerach Nebulo]]. In Purewater the order's hand is the mage **Lythande**, who stands at [[Marisette]]'s side: cold, predatory, and powerful enough to drop a man unconscious with a word. The order may intervene directly against the Baron, and is a likely source of counsel on the possession ritual and the demon afflicting [[Emmeralda]].
