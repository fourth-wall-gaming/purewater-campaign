A close-knit, austere order of knights sworn to a code of honor beyond the city watch or the King's army, and held to account by the goddess herself. They hold battle honors from the ancient uprising against the Dragon Kings and live disciplined lives of service. They operate across Mystamyr to protect the realm and, above all, the old water magic.

Their most renowned member is [[Temerach Nebulo]], "the Lakelady's Javelin" -- an elven knight of legendary skill with bow and spear, a rare outsider who rose to greatness among them. She has entered the Lake Lady's Tourney's archery competition not for glory but to track the Baron and the "Awake the Dragon" movement, and to shield [[Conall]], whose parentage she has long known.

**Stated aim:** safety of the realm. **Real aim:** expose and stop the Baron's revival of draconic power before it can take root. They have resources, agents, and dead-drops throughout Purewater, and move through quiet, unknown intermediaries.
