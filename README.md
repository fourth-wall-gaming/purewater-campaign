# And Then the Dragons Came: Purewater

A faithful **Classic Fantasy Imperative (CFI)** port of the campaign *And then the
Dragons Came*, relocated from the city of Zorastor to **Purewater** — a Venice-like
city of canals where the sacred waters of the Lake Lady meet the sea. Baron Hanzo
di Teufel arrives with his Dragon Knights for the Lake Lady's Tourney, his possessed
champion hidden among them, and four strangers tied by fate are drawn into stopping
him.

A **Mythras / Classic Fantasy** campaign in the
[mythras-gm](https://github.com/fourth-wall-gaming/mythras-gm) publishable
campaign format (v1.0).

## The setting in one breath

Purewater is rich, beautiful, and rotten — locals call it "Pisswater." Power is
split between a distant Governor, the merchant princes, the water-faith of the
**Lake Lady**, the guilds and harbor masters, the broker **Ravella**, and
**Crowbill**'s coded underworld. Beneath everything runs one elemental conflict:
the city's **pure water magic** against the **fire demonology** seeping from Baron
Hanzo's **DragonBarge**, steam rising wherever the two meet.

## The companions

- **Gardwen** — druid (CFI Cleric of the old ways), sole survivor of a Dragon
  Knight massacre, searching a decade for her stolen brother; wolf companion Ruhi.
- **Magda** — an old warrior of **Hel**, sent by the death-goddess's nightmares to
  protect the others and "lay the dragon warrior to rest."
- **Randall** — Purewater street-thief (CFI Rogue), framed for Santo's crime
  because he wears the Baron's son's face.
- **Conall** — arcane investigator (CFI Magic-User), Randall's identical twin, and
  — unknown to him at first — Baron Hanzo's son.
- **Temerach Nebulo** — elven knight of the Swords of the Lake, the party's patron.

## Source & attribution

This is a setting conversion + CFI port of *And then the Dragons Came* (originally
set in Zorastor, world of Greymoor). The plot, characters, relationships, and
themes are preserved; geography, gods, and several institutions are relocated to
the water-bound city of Purewater. See
[`setting/conversion-from-zorastor.md`](setting/conversion-from-zorastor.md).
