Purewater is built across many islands linked by canals and bridges, each with its own character (see the individual location entries):

- **[[High Isle]]** -- official power and the Lake Lady's Tourney grounds; ringed by the Grand Canal; the Baron's [[The DragonBarge]] at its eastern pier.
- **Merchant's Isle** -- guild halls, trade houses, the merchant princes.
- **[[Temple Isle]]** -- the Order of the Lake Lady and the Sacred Confluence.
- **[[Forge Isle]]** -- Dwarven crafters and metalwork.
- **[[The Warren]]** -- the poor; heartland of the Dredgers.
- **[[The Shoals]]** -- docks, fishers, smugglers.
- **[[Lost Isle]]** -- sunken ruins; the Mermaid's Court below.
- **The Pleasure Islands / [[The Pearl Quarter]]** -- the houses of Marisette's network, including [[The Sylph's Embrace]] and [[The Siren's Call]].
- **[[The Maze]]** -- the thieves' tangle, with [[The Moist Oyster]].
- **[[The Catacombs]]** -- ancient tunnels and primordial pools beneath it all.

Gondolas and boats carry everything; the canals are the streets. Distinctive set-pieces the geography enables: canal chases, the tournament's **Harbor Run** obstacle course, and the steam-wreathed corruption spreading from the DragonBarge.
