## The shape of Purewater

Purewater is not a ring of districts around a centre -- it is a city built across
**the Mouth**, the narrow strait where the sacred **Lake** (to the **north**) drains
south into the **open Sea**. Hold that picture and the rest follows.

**The two jaws of the Mouth.**
- From the **western** mainland, a thin **promontory** reaches east to a larger
  outcrop: **[[Caravan Square]]** and the Gate of Triumph -- the only way into the
  city by land. This is the southern jaw.
- A second **peninsula**, set a little north and east, forms the seaward jaw and
  carries **[[The Shoals]]** -- the docks, fish-markets and smugglers' jetties that
  face the open sea.

**[[High Isle]]**, the heart, is a great irregular **triangular** island wedged
between the jaws, splitting the Lake's outflow. Its **south-west** point reaches
toward Caravan Square (the calmer landward channel); its **east** point toward the
Shoals (the seaward channel, the true Mouth); its **north-west** point faces the open
Lake. The **Grand Canal bisects and branches across it**, dividing its quarters:

- **[[The Merchant's Quarter]]** (south-west) -- trade, guilds, the Watercrafters,
  the merchant princes.
- **The official & noble quarter** (north-east) -- the Governor's Palazzo
  ([[Prince Emeric]]), Hall of Justice, Barracks, Palace Square, and the wealthy.
- **[[The Forge Quarter]]** & industry (east) -- forges and yards spilling into the
  Shoals; the Baron's **[[The DragonBarge]]** at its seaward pier.
- **The residential north edge** -- sloping rich-to-middling south-east toward the
  canal-mouth, beyond which lies **[[The Lullwater]]**, the poor-and-criminal quarter
  (the old "Maze" and "Warren" combined; its calm name a deliberate mask).

**[[The Northern Isles]]**, scattered across the Lake's calmer waters north of High
Isle: **[[The Pearl]]** (pleasure and nightlife), **[[Lost Isle]]** (drowned ruins;
the [[The Mermaid's Court]] beneath), quiet **court-family retreats**, and a few
**shunned or haunted** islets. Off the north-east edge, alone and unbridged, sits
little **[[Temple Isle]]** with its temple and the **Sacred Confluence**.

Boats are the streets; bridges stitch it together; **[[The Catacombs]]** run beneath
it all. The set-pieces the geography invites: canal chases through the Lullwater, the
Harbor Run across the Mouth, and the steam-wreathed corruption spreading from the
DragonBarge on the seaward channel.
