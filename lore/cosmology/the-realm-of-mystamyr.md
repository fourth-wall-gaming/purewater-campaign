Purewater is a city of the realm of **Mystamyr**, a kingdom ruled by **King Halvern** from the capital, **Sharna**. It is an old land that remembers the **Dragon Kings** and the uprising that threw them down -- a memory that makes the Baron's dragon heraldry, openly flaunted, both strange and ominous to those who notice.

Beyond the settled heart of the kingdom lie the **borderlands**, a lawless march of forests, swamps ("kritch"), and scattered villages where raids, pogroms, and reprisals have run unchecked for thirty years. It is here that [[Baron Hanzo di Teufel]] rules as a sorcerous tyrant -- enslaving, murdering, exterminating elves, and committing atrocities always with enough deniability that King Halvern, far away in Sharna, suspects nothing. He is, in [[Temerach Nebulo]]'s judgment, the greatest existing threat to Mystamyr and the King's reign.

Purewater itself sits at the kingdom's watery edge, the gate between inland lake and open sea. It is governed in the King's name by his young nephew, **[[Prince Emeric]]** -- clever, idealistic, and hopelessly unsuited to a city like this one, where every faction runs circles around his good intentions. The smaller towns of the march -- **Anminster**, where [[Conall]] was raised by a militiaman's family; the woodland grove where [[Gardwen]]'s people were massacred -- feed travelers and tragedy alike toward the city and its tournament.

*(Setting note: this is the Purewater conversion of the original campaign, which set the same story in the southern city of Zorastor in the world of Greymoor. Names, gods, and a few institutions were relocated; the plot, characters, and themes are preserved.)*
