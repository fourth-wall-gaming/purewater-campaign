**GM ONLY.** The adventure runs on a tournament clock:

- **3 days before the tourney** -- [[Santo di Teufel]]'s botched attempt to bind a pleasure demon into the courtesan [[Emmeralda]] at [[The Sylph's Embrace]]; the soul-knife is abandoned at the scene; [[Gardwen]]'s healing saves the girl from death and full possession. The PCs' entry point.
- **2 days before** -- Santo seeks to recover the knife: theft attempt, then a public confrontation with [[Constantine]] on the Pearl street, then threats of arson -- ended when [[Blau]] hauls him back to his father. Emmeralda's demon-taint surfaces.
- **1 day before** -- [[Nus]] pickpockets the Baron's **binding locket** at tournament registration, on [[Conall]]'s behalf. The locket is hidden away.
- **Post-theft** -- the Baron discovers the loss and launches a purge to recover it; **Nus is killed and his body displayed as a warning**. The Baron grows desperate.
- **Tournament opening** -- [[Cailan]]/Thuban appears as the Baron's champion in the Single Combat; **Gardwen recognizes her brother**, and the campaign reaches its climax.

The trap the party plans -- baiting Santo with the rumor that Emmeralda will be exorcised in the woods, to recover his knife and corner him -- runs alongside this clock and is the players' first proactive move.
