**GM reference -- the cast at a glance.** (Full sheets in `characters/`.)

**The companions:** [[Gardwen]] (druid/Cleric, seeking her brother; wolf [[Ruhi]]) - [[Magda]] (old warrior of Hel, sent by visions) - [[Randall]] (street-thief, framed, secret son of Hanzo) - [[Conall]] (arcane investigator, Randall's twin, secret son of Hanzo) - [[Temerach Nebulo]] (elven knight of the Swords of the Lake, the party's patron and Conall's protector).

**The di Teufel faction:** [[Baron Hanzo di Teufel]] (fire-demonologist, father of the twins, the great threat) - [[Santo di Teufel]] (his cruel legitimate heir; carved Emmeralda) - [[Tat Atarer]] (mercenary captain of the Dragon Knights) - [[Blau]]/Commander Vask (albino enforcer-lieutenant) - [[Cailan]] (the possessed champion, Gardwen's brother) - [[Thuban Eta]] (the bound spirit within him).

**Purewater powers:** [[Marisette]] (madame ruling the Pearl Quarter) - [[Constantine]] (Sylph's Embrace) - [[Ravella]] (half-merfolk broker, Siren's Call) - [[Crowbill]] (crime lord with a code) - [[Thorne]] (storyteller, ex-Order of the Lake, lore-keeper) - [[High Priestess Nerissa]] & [[Lilura Deepcurrent]] (the Lake Lady's clergy) - [[Guildmaster Torval Bluehand]] (Watercrafters) - [[Marda Blackwater]] (Dredgers) - [[Harbormaster Velen Deepkeel]] (Harbor Masters) - the mage **[[Hesper]]** (sorcerer-adept of the [[The Brotherhood of the Veiled Star]], at Marisette's side) - **[[Prince Emeric]]** (the young, idealistic Governor of Purewater, the King's nephew -- well-meaning and out of his depth).

**The afflicted & the help:** [[Emmeralda]] (demon-tainted courtesan, evidence and victim) - [[Asphodel]] & [[Mariarta]] (courtesans of the Promise of Heaven; Randall's confidantes and informants) - [[Nus]] (pickpocket, Randall's underworld ally) - [[Sinnit]] (Maze thug, reluctant ally) - plus the offscreen mentors in `supporting-and-offscreen-cast`.
