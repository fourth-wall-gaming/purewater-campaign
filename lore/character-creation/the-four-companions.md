The campaign opens by drawing four (with Temerach, five) strangers together in the days before the tourney:

- **[[Gardwen]]**, sole survivor of a Dragon Knight massacre a decade past, follows the Baron's column to Purewater seeking the brother who vanished that night. Her wolf [[Ruhi]] walks beside her; the old powers keep her footfalls silent.
- **[[Magda]]**, an old pig-farmer and warrior of **Hel**, is sent by the death-goddess's nightmares to protect the others and "lay the dragon warrior to rest." She finds Gardwen at the city's ford and becomes her curmudgeonly bodyguard.
- **[[Randall]]**, a Purewater street-thief raised in the Pearl Quarter, is mistaken for the noble's son **Santo** at the city gate -- and then framed for the carving of [[Emmeralda]].
- **[[Conall]]**, an arcane investigator trained by the [[The Order of the Blue Star]] and riding in with the knight [[Temerach Nebulo]], is Randall's identical twin -- and, unknown to him at first, the Baron's son.

They meet bound to chairs in [[The Catacombs]], suspected of Emmeralda's murder, until Magda's testimony and Randall's account of "Lord Santo" clear them. Over the soul-knife Conall recovers from the crime scene, four strangers tied by fate forge an alliance: *"We could. We should." -- "Justice."* The twins' shared parentage, and Conall's, unspool from there.
